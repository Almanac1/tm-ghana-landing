About your Instructor

Ojemewanlan Roselyn Okosun is a self-motivated corporate executive with over a decade of professional experience across West Africa. With an MBA from Accra Business School, she brings a sharp understanding of high-pressure work environments and the need for practical, effective tools for managing stress. Today she teaches Transcendental Meditation as a simple, science-backed practice for achieving inner peace, emotional balance, and self-awareness. Roselyn is passionate about helping busy professionals reconnect with themselves, manage their stress, and become more productive and creative.
